About Photos By Abby

Abbigail Merrill is a freelance photographer, freelance web developer, and a full time student at IUPUI. Abbigail has her bachelor's in Media Arts and Sciences with a specialization in Web Development, and is working on obtaining her Master's in Media Arts and Sciences as well.

Abbigail is the founder of Photos by Abby and began her senior year of college at IUPUI. She travels to events like weddings and livestock shows, and takes photos as requested, with her few friends that are members of Photos by Abby. Abby then edits them herself using Adobe Creative Suite.
